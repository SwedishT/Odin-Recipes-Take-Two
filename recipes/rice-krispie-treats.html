Rice Krispie Treats

These simple rice krispie treats will make you snap, crackle and POP!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of rice krispies cereal
  • 1 bag of mini-marshmellows
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Melt your butter and marshmellows in a medium saucepot over medium-low heat.
  2. Once melted, melted mixture to a large bowl with all of your rice krispies. Add vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  3. Spread rice krispie mixture across a greased baking pan.
  4. Let pan cool for an hour at room temperature, then cut and serve!

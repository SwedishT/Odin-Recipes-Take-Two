Chicken Ceasar Salad

This chicken cesar salad is incredible! Using greek yogurt, this salad is packed with proteins and low carb.

Ingredients

  • 3 heads of romaine lettuce
  • 1.5lbs chicken breast
  • 3/4 parmesean cheese
  • 1 bag of store bought croutons
  • 1 cup of non-fat greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons anchovy paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 garlic clove
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook chicken in large skillet over medium-high heat, until browned and cooked all the way through. Let rest for 5min and then slice into 1" strips.
  2. Chop lettuce into bite size piece and add to large bowl. Shave 1/2 cup parmesean into bowl and add all of the chicken, mix thouroghly.
  3. Into medium bowl, add yogurt, remaining 1/4 cup cheese, anchovy paste, olive oil, lemon juice and minced garlic clove. Stir well to combine. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve and then add sauce as desired!

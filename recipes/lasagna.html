Lasagna

This is my famous lasagna recipe! Everytime I make this recipe, there are no leftovers. Besides being tasty, this recipe is high in protein and is loaded with vegetables. See below for full recipe!

Ingredients

  • 15 lasagna noodles
  • 1lb Lean ground beef
  • 3/4 cup button mushrooms
  • 2 zuchini
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 celery stalks
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of part skim mozzerella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in 6qts of boiling water. *Don't forget to salt your water! Cook to pacakge instructions and drain.
  3. In a large skillet, cook your beef in olive oil until well browned. Remove from stove and set aside.
  4. To same skillet add your all of your vegetables. Add salt and cook until all of the water moisture has been cooked out.
  5. Combine vegetables and beef together.
  6. In a 8x20 baking dish, lay down 1/2 a cup of your pasta sauce. Layer on top 5 lasagna noodles. On top of the noodles, spread half of the vegetable/beef mixture. On top of the mixture, add 1/2 a cup of cheese.
  7. Repeat this step from the beginning until all of the noodles have been utilized
  8. Bake for 25 minutes and until the top layer of cheese is browned
